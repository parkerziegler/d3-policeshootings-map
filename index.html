How are Shootings
Distributed Over
the Course of the Year?

Examining the data reveals no distinct temporal trends in police involved shootings.

In the time period surveyed, July 2015 and March 2015 had the most shootings of any month
at 124 and 113, respectively. June 2015 and April 2016 tied for the least, each with 80 shootings.
Perhaps the most troubling aspect of this visualization is that there have only been 27 days
in this 556-day time period in which no police involved shootings occurred.
